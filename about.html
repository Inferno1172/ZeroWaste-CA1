My Favorite Green Hacks!

I love experimenting with low-waste hacks that are simple, effective, and sometimes a little unexpected!

  • Coffee Grounds for Plants

    I sprinkle used grounds around my garden as fertilizer. Bonus: slugs hate it!

  • Citrus Peel Cleaners

    Soak orange and lemon peels in vinegar for two weeks to create a fresh-smelling all-purpose cleaner.

  • Beeswax Wraps

    I make my own food wraps with fabric scraps and beeswax to ditch single-use plastic wrap.

  • Organic Waste Composter

    I collect organic waste like egg shells and banana peels and use it to make my own compost for plants.

  • Investing in reusables

    Although the initial cost is high, the number of single used plastics that can be saved makes it an excellent investment!

It's these little daily hacks that keep me grounded and remind me that sustainability can be joyful, personal, and deeply rewarding.
